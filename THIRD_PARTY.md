# Third-party projects

This project orchestrates two open-source crawlers/exporters:

| Project | Purpose | Install path |
|---|---|---|
| NanmiCoder/MediaCrawler | Xiaohongshu and Weibo crawling | `third_party/MediaCrawler` |
| wechat-article/wechat-article-exporter | WeChat official account article search/export | `third_party/wechat-article-exporter` |

`setup.ps1` clones both projects from GitHub. Their original licenses remain with their repositories.

Links:

- https://github.com/NanmiCoder/MediaCrawler
- https://github.com/wechat-article/wechat-article-exporter
